# Oppgaver: <br> $abc$-formelen

:::::{admonition} Oppsummering: $abc$-formelen
---
class: summary
---
Når en andregradslikning er skrevet på formen 

$$
ax^2 + bx + c = 0,
$$

er løsningen

$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}.
$$

:::::

---

:::::::::::::::{admonition} Oppgave 1
---
class: problem-level-1
---
Løs likningene ved bruk av $abc$-formelen.

::::::::::::::{tab-set}
---
class: tabs-parts
---

:::::::::::::{tab-item} a
$$
x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0
$$

:::{admonition} Fasit
---
class: answer, dropdown
---
$$
x = 2 \, \lor \, x = 3
$$
:::
:::::::::::::

:::::::::::::{tab-item} b
$$
x^2 - 3x - 4 = 0
$$

:::{admonition} Fasit
---
class: answer, dropdown
---
$$
x \in \{-1, 4\}
$$
:::
:::::::::::::

:::::::::::::{tab-item} c
$$
x^2 - x - 6 = 0
$$

:::{admonition} Fasit
---
class: answer, dropdown
---
$$
x = -2 \, \lor \, x = 3
$$
:::
:::::::::::::

:::::::::::::{tab-item} d
$$
x^2 - 4x - 5 = 0
$$

:::{admonition} Fasit
---
class: answer, dropdown
---
$$
x = -1 \, \lor \, x = 5
$$
:::

$$
