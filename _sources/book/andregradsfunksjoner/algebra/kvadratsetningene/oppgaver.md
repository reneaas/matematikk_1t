:::::::::::::::{admonition} Oppgave 3
---
class: problem-level-2
---
Bestem de ukjente koeffisientene slik at sammenhengene under blir **identiteter**. 

::::::::::::::{tab-set}
---
class: tabs-parts
---
:::::::::::::{tab-item} a
$$
(x + b)^2 = x^2 + 6x + 9
$$
:::::::::::::

:::::::::::::{tab-item} b
$$
(ax + b)^2 = 16x^2 + 8x + 1
$$
:::::::::::::

:::::::::::::{tab-item} c
$$
(2x - 2)^2 = ax^2 + bx + c
$$
:::::::::::::

:::::::::::::{tab-item} d
$$
(ax + b)(ax - b) = 9x^2 - 4
$$
:::::::::::::
