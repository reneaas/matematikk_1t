**Matematisk logikk og symboler**
^^^
$$
-2 < x \leq 4 \liff x \in \langle -2, 4]
$$

$$
x^2 = 4 \liff x = 2 \or x = -2
$$

$$
x = 3 \limplies x^2 = 9
$$


$$
x = 2 \and y = 3 \limplies x + y = 5
$$
