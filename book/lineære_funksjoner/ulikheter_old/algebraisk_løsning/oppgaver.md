# Oppgaver: <br> Algebraisk løsning av lineære ulikheter

::::::::::::{admonition} Oppgave 1 
---
class: problem-level-1
---
I hver av oppgavene skal du uttrykke løsningen som en *ulikhet*.

:::::::::::{tab-set}
---
class: tabs-parts
---
::::::::::{tab-item} a
Løs ulikheten 

$$
x - 1 > 0
$$

:::{admonition} Fasit
---
class: answer, dropdown
---
$$
x > 1
$$
:::

::::::::::

::::::::::{tab-item} b
Løs ulikheten

$$
2x - 3 \geq 1
$$

:::{admonition} Fasit
---
class: answer, dropdown
---
$$
x \geq 2
$$
:::

::::::::::

::::::::::{tab-item} c

Løs ulikheten 

$$
-x - 2 > 2x - 1
$$

:::{admonition} Fasit
---
class: answer, dropdown
---
$$
x < -\dfrac{1}{3}
$$
:::

::::::::::

::::::::::{tab-item} d
Løs ulikheten 

$$
2x - 3 \leq -x + 5
$$

:::{admonition} Fasit
---
class: answer, dropdown
---
$$
x \leq \dfrac{8}{3}
$$

::::::::::::{admonition} Oppgave 2 
---
class: problem-level-1
---
I hver av oppgavene skal du uttrykke løsningen som en *løsningsmengde*.

:::::::::::{tab-set}
---
class: tabs-parts
---
::::::::::{tab-item} a
Løs ulikheten

$$
x + 1 > 2x + 4
$$


:::{admonition} Fasit
---
class: answer, dropdown
---
$$
x \in \langle \gets, -3 \rangle
$$
:::

::::::::::

::::::::::{tab-item} b
Løs ulikheten

$$
x + 1 < -x + 2
$$

:::{admonition} Fasit
---
class: answer, dropdown
---
$$
x \in \left\langle \gets, \dfrac{1}{2} \right\rangle
$$
:::

::::::::::

::::::::::{tab-item} c
Løs ulikheten

$$
1 - x \leq 1 + x
$$

:::{admonition} Fasit
---
class: answer, dropdown
---
$$
x \in [0, \to\rangle
$$
:::

::::::::::

::::::::::{tab-item} d
Løs ulikheten

$$
-3x + 5 \geq \dfrac{1}{2}x + 3
$$

:::{admonition} Fasit
---
class: answer, dropdown
---
$$
x \in \left[\dfrac{4}{7} ,\to\right\rangle
$$
:::
