Here is an example of an annotated equation using the `annotate-equations` package:

\begin{equation}
    \annotated{a^2 + b^2 = c^2}{Pythagorean theorem}
\end{equation}

\annotateeqn{
    a^2  & \annotate*{term}{Represents one of the legs of the right triangle} \\
    b^2  & \annotate*{term}{Represents the other leg of the right triangle} \\
    c^2  & \annotate*{term}{Represents the hypotenuse of the triangle}
}
