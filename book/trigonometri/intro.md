$\sin v$, $\cos v$ og $\tan v$


^^^

\begin{align*}
    \sin \angle A &= \dfrac{\mathrm{mot}}{\mathrm{hyp}} \\
    \cos \angle A &= \dfrac{\mathrm{hos}}{\mathrm{hyp}} \\
    \tan \angle A &= \dfrac{\mathrm{mot}}{\mathrm{hos}}
\end{align*}

**Arealsetningen**


^^^

\begin{align*}
    T &= \dfrac{1}{2} bc \sin A \\
    T &= \dfrac{1}{2} ac \sin B \\
    T &= \dfrac{1}{2} ab \sin C \\
\end{align*}
