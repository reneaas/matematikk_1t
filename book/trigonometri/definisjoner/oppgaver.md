:::::::::::::::{admonition} Oppgave 9
---
class: problem-level-3
---
Nedenfor vises noen påstander for rettvinklede trekanter.

Avgjør om påstandene stemmer eller ikke.


::::::::::::::{tab-set}
---
class: tabs-parts
---
:::::::::::::{tab-item} a
For en rettvinklet trekant, gjelder alltid 

$$
\sin v = \cos u
$$

der $u + v = 90^\circ$. 

::::{admonition} Fasit
---
class: answer, dropdown
---
Påstanden stemmer.
::::


:::::::::::::

:::::::::::::{tab-item} b
For en rettvinklet trekant, gjelder alltid 

$$

$$

der $u + v = 90^\circ$. 


::::{admonition} Fasit
---
class: answer, dropdown
---
Påstanden stemmer.
::::

:::::::::::::

$$


::::{admonition} Fasit
